Interactive Process Flow

Hover over steps to highlight the corresponding architecture components and data flows.

1

Trade & Submission

The Vendor (Supplier) submits the initial invoice into the Cortex platform.

2

Anchor Confirmation

Data syncs to Verity. The Anchor (Buyer) confirms the invoice, creating a programmable, immutable liability.

3

Factoring Initiation

The Factor (Investor) steps in to refinance the confirmed invoice on the Verity platform.

4

Payment Instructions

Factoring generates underlying instruction sets sent to the CLOB (Advance to Vendor, Repayment from Anchor).

5

Swap Accounting & Netting

The Facilitator Netting Engine routes funds via a Swap Accounting Journal. This ensures an immutable audit trail for every leg before final AP/AR wallet settlement.
