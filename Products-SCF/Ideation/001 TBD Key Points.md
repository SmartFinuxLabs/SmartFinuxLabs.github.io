
Supplier Alignment:
    Have suppliers (SMEs) explicitly accept secondary liability if the Buyer defaults. The can be enforced have "Advanced Payment" termed as conditional loan agreements.
    When buyer make the final payment, the condition term is fulfilled and the collateral is released to the supplier. 
    If the buyer defaults, the collateral can be liquidated to repay investors, with pre-conditioned interest rates to compensate for the delay and risk. 
    This creates a strong incentive for suppliers to only work with reliable buyers, as they are also on the hook for defaults.
