
# Smart Finux Labs

Smart Finux Labs is focused on modernizing how value, accounting, and settlement
work across digital and traditional financial systems.

## Overview

### Description

We are building a commerce, supply chain, and financial infrastructure suite
under Smart Finux, designed to transform how business obligations, accounting
truth, and payment settlement converge.

Modern commerce depends on:

Sourcing -> Producing -> Storage -> Trading -> Transportation -> Payment ->
Settlement

Our product suite explores how programmable finance infrastructure can support
real-world business processes such as invoicing, receivables, settlement,
treasury coordination, and cross-border value movement.
